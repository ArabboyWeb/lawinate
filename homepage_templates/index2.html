Why it works

Bu variant eng React-friendly

Hamma sectionlar alohida componentga tabiiy bo'linadi: nav, hero-copy, hero-bento, metrics, social proof va CTA. Keyinchalik motion qo'shish, dark mode yoki CMS content bilan ishlash ham oson bo'ladi.

  • Product-led hero: sahifa darrov mahsulotni ko'rsatadi.
  • Clean hierarchy: heading, stats va cards bir-birini bosmaydi.
  • Modern visual language: light glass, bento grid va crisp spacing.
